## Phrases to Use & Avoid

### ✅ Power Phrases (Use These)

| Phrase | Effect |
|---|---|
| "Here's what the data shows..." | Anchors discussion in facts |
| "What would you need to see to move forward?" | Uncovers their real objection |
| "I'm flexible on structure, firm on value." | Shows openness, maintains position |
| "Let's solve this together." | Collaborative tone |
| "The opportunity cost of waiting is..." | Creates urgency |
| "What's your biggest concern here?" | Surfaces real issue |
| "I see two paths forward..." | Gives them control (false choice) |

### ❌ Avoid These (Weak Language)

| Never Say | Why Bad | Say Instead |
|---|---|---|
| "I need this money" | Shows desperation | "This represents fair market value" |
| "Is that okay?" | Seeks approval | "Does that make sense?" |
| "I'll take whatever you offer" | No leverage | "What feels fair to you?" |
| "That's my final offer" | Painted into corner | "That's where I'd like to start" |
| "I guess..." | Shows doubt | "Based on the data..." |
| "Maybe we could..." | Weak proposal | "I propose we..." |
